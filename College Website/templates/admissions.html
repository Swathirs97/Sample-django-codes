Eligibility Criteria

Meet the requirements for admission

Undergraduate Programs

  • Completion of higher secondary education (10+2) or equivalent
  • Minimum 60% aggregate marks in qualifying examination
  • Valid entrance exam scores (if applicable)

Postgraduate Programs

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant field from recognized university
  • Minimum 55% aggregate marks in undergraduate program
  • Valid entrance exam scores (GATE/CAT/GRE as applicable)
